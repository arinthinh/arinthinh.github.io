Hi, I'm Pham Phuc Thinh.

I am a 25-year-old Unity Developer based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. Over the past 4 years, I have been working on mobile games, primarily focusing on gameplay development for puzzle and hyper-casual titles.

I consider myself a gameplay-focused programmer who enjoys turning ideas into interactive and engaging experiences. I pay close attention to player experience and enjoy polishing small details that come together to create a satisfying game overall. For me, making games has always been more exciting than playing them.

I particularly enjoy solving gameplay challenges and designing logic systems that make mechanics feel intuitive and rewarding. Whenever I encounter a difficult problem, I prefer digging into it myself and exploring different solutions until I find the one that feels right. The process of building, iterating, and refining gameplay is what keeps me motivated and constantly learning.
